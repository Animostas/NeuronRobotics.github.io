Getting Started

Novice Users

If you're interested in electronics as a hobby and just don't know where to get started then this section is for you. We provide strong descriptions of the software and hardware that can help you get started on exciting projects. We will attempt to break down the more complicated language into its most simplistic form and guide you through with step by step instructions so you can enjoy the fruits of your labor right away!
